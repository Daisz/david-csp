Big Data Research

In this 'project,' which was really a presentation, I made a slide with information regarding "Big Data," and explained how that can affect users while shopping at Amazon.

What the Algorithm does:
The algorithm makes note of which websites users visit, and what items they view. Using this information, the algorithm can make an informed decision as to what products the user might also be interested in, and those produts are advertised to the user. This data can be categorized under "Volume," since a company like Amazon would have to process massive amounts of data considering how many customers they serve, which all require personalized data in order to help Amazon make more sales.

What I learned while researching this topic, is how companies like Amazon process data, and how they use that data in order to provide a better shopping experience to their users.
